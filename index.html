Professional Translation Services

Since 1987, corporations, non-profit organizations, law firms, and government entities of all sizes have relied on ASTA-USA Translation Services, Inc. to translate important documents, correspondence, and other written materials. As a highly specialized language servicesprovider, our expertise in intricate sectors is unrivaled. Whether legal, scientific, financial, or technical, our rigorous vetting process demands industry-specific knowledge and a minimum of 10 years of translation experience. By curating a network of subject matter expert (SME) linguists, we can accurately replicate each translation from the source language into over 70 languages. This innate ability, paired with our company-wide devotion to confidentiality, reliability, timeliness, and overall customer satisfaction results in simple, yet impeccable professional translation services.

Legal Translation Services

Our certified legal translators speak more than 70 languages—and legalese. Because language and the law are so closely interwoven, each one of ASTA-USA’s legal linguists is also an attorney with 10 or more years of experience. This enables them to be well-versed in the intricacies and statutory requirements of domestic law, as well as foreign legal and cultural systems. Whether your legal document translations involve critical business contracts, complex litigation materials, evidence documents, financial statements, deposition records, technical patent confirmations, application letters, birth certificates, or any other essential documents, rest assured that each one remains air-tight in any court. By leaning on subject matter experts, translations can be curated quickly and with precision to meet any pressing court deadlines. In fact, in more than 35 years, our work has never been challenged by a single client, court, or government entity.

Translations for legal professionals—by legal professionals.

*ASTA-USA Translation Services Inc. is a proud preferred partner of the National Association for Legal Support Professionals (NALS).
